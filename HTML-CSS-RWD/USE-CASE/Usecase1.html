Quick hummons Recipe

The recipe makes quick tasty hummons with no messing. It has been adapted from a number of different recipes that i have read over the year.
Hummons is a delecious thick paste used heavily in Greek and middle Eastern dishes. It is very tasty with salad grilled meats and pitta breads

Ingredients

  • 1 can(400gm) of chick peas
  • 1.5 Kg of tahini
  • 6 unwashed tomatoes
  • Half a red pepper
  • A pinch of cinnemon powder
  • 1 clove of garlic
  • A dash of olive oil

Instruction

  1. Remove the skin from the garlic and chop coaresly
  2. Remove all the seeds and stalk from the pepper chop coaresly
  3. Add all the ingredients in a food processor
  4. Process all the ingredients in a paste
  5. If you want a bit chunky hummons then process it for small time
  6. If you want a smooth hummons process it for a long time

For a different flavour you could try blending a small lemon.
